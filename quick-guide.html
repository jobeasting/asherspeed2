Equipment needed
Flat surface — driveway, park, or basketball court (~20 yds)
3–5 cones or small markers
Low step or box, 6–12 inches (weeks 3, 5, 6 only)
Water bottle · Athletic shoes
Key coaching principles
Landing quality before load — soft, controlled, knees over toes
Full recovery between max-effort sprints (60–90 sec minimum)
Week 4 deload is mandatory — adaptation happens in recovery
Sleep 9+ hours — most important recovery tool at this age
